I realized several things visiting the 'Affirming my Values' blog post from way back in Week 1 of DBC Prep.


For one, my page design is much improved, thank goodness. Also, I gotta edit my blog posts.


But ok - staying on topic. I rated many values that I found most important in the choices I make. I rated MANY values. I'll focus on those I rated most highly for myself.


When I see this list of values laid out - I notice a tension between the desire to push myself as an individual, and the desire to create + cultivate community.


Individual Focus Community Focus
Accomplishment Accountability
Autonomy Affection
Competence Close relationships
Courage Community
Creativity Fairness


I hope that having this tension can help inform my actions, and allow for a happy medium as a community member of DBC. We're all here because we have a desire for personal accomplishment. AND, we're all here together. A dog eat dog mentality will not help anyone. And happily, DBC seems to work really hard to encourage collaboration.
